Ines Arous

Ines Arous is a Ph.D. student in the eXascale Infolab group at the university of Fribourg in Switzerland
starting from March 2017.

She holds an engineering degree from SUPCOM school in Tunisia. She took part of an Erasmus MSc program in 2016 at University of Padova in Italy.

Her main research interests include large time series analysis, prediction models and entity extraction.
