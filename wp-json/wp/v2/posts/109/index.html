The launch of the Big Data Value PPP<\/span> Steering<\/span> Committee! Brussels, 13th March<\/h3>\n

Alessandro Checco represented the FashionBrain Project at the launch of the Big Data Value PPP Steering Committee in Brussels on the 13th March 2017.<\/p>\n

The main objectives of the meeting was to launch the governance structure, get to know to each other, provide an informal opportunity to get all the projects of the Big Data Value PPP portfolio around the same table, understand the context of the PPP and some of the general activities to which projects are expected to contribute, such as the monitoring\/KPI framework and a joint communication strategy.<\/p>\n

Representatives from the following were invited to make an overall project presentation<\/p>\n
