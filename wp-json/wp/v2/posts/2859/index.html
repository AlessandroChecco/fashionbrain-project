MonetDB Solutions Secures an Investment from ServiceNow to help Large Enterprises Drive Digital Transformation at Scale<\/p>\n

AMSTERDAM, October 1, 2019<\/p>\n

MonetDB Solutions, a company formed by researchers of the Dutch National Research Institute for Mathematics and Computer Science (CWI), today announced an investment from digital workflow company ServiceNow to help large enterprises drive digital transformation. Additionally, the two companies will work together to build MonetDB open-source database technology into ServiceNow\u2019s Now Platform to strengthen analytics capabilities and to drive faster transaction speeds for its largest customers.<\/p>\n

\u201cIn today\u2019s world of digital business, largeapproach to database technology,\u201d said Prof. dr. Martin Kersten, CEO and co-founder, MonetDB Solutions, \u201cTogether with ServiceNow, we will continue to build out our Database-as-a-Service environment, as MonetDB also plans to invest in further product innovation.\u201d<\/p>\n

\u201cWe partner with some of the largest organizations in the world to deliver digital workflows that create great experiences and unlock productivity,\u201d said Pat Casey, chief technology officer and senior vice president of DevOps at ServiceNow. \u201cOur priority is to make the Now Platform the most innovative for our customers to get work done. We invested in MonetDB to share our expertise in cloud automation and drive greater benefits for some of the world\u2019s largest companies.\u201d<\/p>\n

About MonetDB Solutions
\nMonetDB Solutions, a spin-off company of the Dutchtechnical development and consulting company of the open-source analytical database system MonetDB, specialised in database technologies for Business Intelligence and Big Data Analytics. In MonetDB Solutions world-leading database researchers and engineers support software companies in developing cutting-edge applications addressing vertical markets, such as telecom, health care and education. For more information, visit www.monetdbsolutions.com<\/a>.<\/p>\n
